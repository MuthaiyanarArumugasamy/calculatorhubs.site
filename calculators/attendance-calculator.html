What is Attendance Percentage?

Attendance percentage shows how many classes you attended out of the total classes conducted.

Formula

Attendance % = (Classes Attended ÷ Total Classes) × 100

Example

Total Classes = 100

Attended = 85

Attendance = (85 ÷ 100) × 100 = 85%
