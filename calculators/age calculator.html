About Age Calculator

The Age Calculator helps you calculate your exact age from your date of birth. It is useful for school admissions, passport applications, job applications and many other situations where your precise age is required.

How to Use

  • Select your date of birth.
  • Click Calculate Age.
  • Your age will be displayed instantly.
