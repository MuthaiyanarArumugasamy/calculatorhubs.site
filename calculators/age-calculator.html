About Age Calculator

The Age Calculator helps you calculate the exact age between two dates in years, months and days. It is useful for students, employees, passport applications, government forms and many other situations.

Why Use This Age Calculator?

Instead of calculating your age manually, simply select your Date of Birth and the date on which you want to calculate your age. The calculator instantly provides accurate results.

This tool is completely free, mobile-friendly and works on all modern devices. It saves time and eliminates calculation mistakes.
