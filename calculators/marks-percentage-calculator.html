What is Marks Percentage?

Marks percentage shows how much you scored out of the total marks in an examination.

Formula

Percentage = (Obtained Marks ÷ Total Marks) × 100

Example

Obtained Marks = 450

Total Marks = 500

Percentage = (450 ÷ 500) × 100 = 90%

Percentage Grade Reference

Percentage Grade

80% - 89% A
