About BMI Calculator

BMI (Body Mass Index) is a simple way to check whether your weight is appropriate for your height. It is commonly used as a health screening tool.

BMI Categories

  • Below 18.5 – Underweight
  • 18.5 to 24.9 – Normal Weight
  • 25 to 29.9 – Overweight
  • 30 and above – Obese
