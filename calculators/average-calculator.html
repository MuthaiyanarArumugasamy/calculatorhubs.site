What is Average?

The average (mean) is the sum of all numbers divided by the total number of values.

Formula

Average = (Sum of Numbers) ÷ (Total Numbers)

Example

Numbers: 10, 20, 30, 40, 50

Sum = 150

Count = 5

Average = 150 ÷ 5 = 30
