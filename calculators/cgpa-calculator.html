What is CGPA?

CGPA (Cumulative Grade Point Average) is the average of all semester GPAs obtained during your course.

Formula

CGPA = Total GPA of All Semesters ÷ Number of Semesters

Example

Semester GPAs: 8.2, 7.8, 9.1, 8.5

CGPA = (8.2 + 7.8 + 9.1 + 8.5) ÷ 4 = 8.40
