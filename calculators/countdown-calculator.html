What is a Countdown Calculator?

A Countdown Calculator calculates the remaining time between the current date and a future date. It displays the result in days, hours, minutes, and seconds.

Example

Today's Date: July 13, 2026

Target Date: December 31, 2026

The calculator will display the remaining days, hours, minutes and seconds.
