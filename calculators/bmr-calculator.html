What is BMR?

Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR) is the number of calories your body burns while at complete rest to maintain basic functions such as breathing, blood circulation and body temperature.

Formula Used

Male
BMR = 10 × Weight + 6.25 × Height − 5 × Age + 5

Female
BMR = 10 × Weight + 6.25 × Height − 5 × Age − 161
