What is GPA?

GPA (Grade Point Average) measures your academic performance for a semester. It is calculated by dividing the total grade points earned by the total credit hours.

Formula

GPA = Total Grade Points ÷ Total Credits

Example

Total Grade Points = 72

Total Credits = 9

GPA = 72 ÷ 9 = 8.00
