How It Works

This calculator estimates your total study hours in a week based on your daily study routine.

Formula

Weekly Study Hours = Study Hours Per Day × Study Days Per Week

Example

Study Hours Per Day = 4

Study Days Per Week = 6

Weekly Study Hours = 4 × 6 = 24 Hours
