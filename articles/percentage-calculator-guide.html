What is Percentage?

A percentage represents a value out of 100. It is one of the most commonly used mathematical concepts in daily life, education, business, finance, and statistics.

Percentage Formula

Percentage = (Value ÷ Total Value) × 100

Example

Marks Obtained Total Marks Percentage
450 500 90%
