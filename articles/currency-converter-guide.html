What is a Currency Converter?

A Currency Converter is an online tool that helps you convert one country's currency into another using the latest exchange rates. It is useful for travelers, online shoppers, businesses, freelancers, students, and anyone dealing with international payments.

How Does a Currency Converter Work?

A currency converter uses the latest exchange rate between two currencies. It multiplies the amount you enter by the current exchange rate to calculate the converted value.

Currency Conversion Formula

Converted Amount = Amount × Exchange Rate

100 USD 1 USD = 86 INR ₹8,600
