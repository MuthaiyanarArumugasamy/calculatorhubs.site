What is CGPA?

CGPA (Cumulative Grade Point Average) is the average of grade points earned across all semesters or subjects. It is widely used by schools, colleges, and universities to measure academic performance.

CGPA Formula

CGPA = Total Grade Points ÷ Total Number of Subjects

Example

Subject Grade Point
Mathematics 9
Science 8
English 9
Computer Science 10
CGPA 9.0

CGPA to Percentage

Many institutions use the following formula:

Percentage = CGPA × 9.5

Example: CGPA = 8.5 Percentage = 8.5 × 9.5 = 80.75%
