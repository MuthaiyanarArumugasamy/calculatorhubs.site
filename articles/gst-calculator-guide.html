What is GST?

GST (Goods and Services Tax) is an indirect tax applied to the sale of goods and services. It replaced many older indirect taxes and simplified the taxation system.

How to Calculate GST?

GST Amount = (Original Price × GST Rate) ÷ 100

Final Price = Original Price + GST Amount

Example

Original Price GST Rate GST Amount Final Price
₹1000 18% ₹180 ₹1180

Common GST Rates

  • 5%
  • 12%
  • 18%
  • 28%
