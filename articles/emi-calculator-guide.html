What is EMI?

EMI (Equated Monthly Installment) is the fixed amount you pay every month to repay a loan. It includes both the principal amount and the interest charged by the lender.

EMI Formula

EMI = P × R × (1+R)N ÷ ((1+R)N − 1)

Where:

  • P = Loan Amount
  • R = Monthly Interest Rate
  • N = Loan Tenure (Months)
