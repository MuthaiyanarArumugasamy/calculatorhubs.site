What is an Age Calculator?

An Age Calculator is a free online tool that helps you calculate your exact age using your date of birth. It displays your age in years, months and days within a few seconds.

It is commonly used for school admissions, passport applications, job applications, insurance forms and many other official purposes.

How Does an Age Calculator Work?

The calculator compares your Date of Birth (DOB) with today's date and calculates the exact difference.

Example

Date of Birth Today's Date Age
15 March 2005 14 July 2026 21 Years 3 Months 29 Days

How to Use an Age Calculator?

  1. Enter your Date of Birth.
  2. Select today's date.
  3. Click the Calculate button.
  4. View your exact age instantly.
