What is a Fixed Deposit (FD)?

A Fixed Deposit (FD) is a savings investment offered by banks and financial institutions where you deposit a fixed amount of money for a specific period. At the end of the tenure, you receive the original amount along with the interest earned.

What is an FD Calculator?

An FD Calculator is an online tool that helps you estimate the maturity amount and interest earned on your Fixed Deposit based on the deposit amount, interest rate, and investment period.

FD Formula

Maturity Amount = Principal + Interest Earned

For compound interest, banks may use different compounding frequencies (monthly, quarterly, or yearly), so the exact maturity amount can vary.
