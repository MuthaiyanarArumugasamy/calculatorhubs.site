What is a Loan Calculator?

A Loan Calculator is a free online financial tool that helps you estimate your monthly loan payments (EMI), total interest payable, and the total repayment amount. It is useful for planning your finances before applying for a loan.

Types of Loans

  • Home Loan
  • Personal Loan
  • Car Loan
  • Education Loan
  • Business Loan
  • Gold Loan

Loan EMI Formula

EMI = P × R × (1 + R)N ÷ ((1 + R)N − 1)

Where:

  • P = Loan Amount
  • R = Monthly Interest Rate
  • N = Loan Tenure (Months)

How to Use the Loan Calculator?

  1. Enter the loan amount.
  2. Enter the annual interest rate.
  3. Select the loan tenure.
  4. Click the Calculate button.
  5. View your monthly EMI, total interest, and total repayment amount.

Tips Before Taking a Loan

  • Compare interest rates from different lenders.
  • Choose the shortest affordable loan tenure.
  • Read all loan terms carefully.
  • Maintain a good credit score.
  • Borrow only the amount you really need.
